Mostafa Emara

Musician | Gamer | Nerd

Hello there. My name is Mostafa, and I've been surrounding myself with music since I was a wee little boy. Since then, I've spent a colossal amount of time both playing drums and video games (more than a decade!). Only recently have I been immersing myself in composing music (with my trusty MIDI keyboard) to go along with video game themes I enjoy. What drives my passion is the feeling of fulfillment I get when crafting a song to match a game.
